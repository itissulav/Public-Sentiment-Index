1. Overall Sentiment Split

Analyst Insight: Provides an immediate macroscopic view of the general public mood. A dominant red slice strongly indicates widespread discontent, while green shows broad approval.

2. Sentiment Timeline (Daily Volume)

Analyst Insight: Traces emotion patterns day-by-day. Use this to pinpoint exactly when the public mood shifted. A sudden viral spike in negative posts directly correlates to a controversial real-world event breaking on that specific date.

3. Average Engagement by Emotion

Analyst Insight: Reveals what type of content the community rewards with upvotes. If the red bar is highest, the platform mathematically is incentivizing users to post outrage and criticism to gain visibility.

4. Positivity vs. Viral Engagement

Analyst Insight: Evaluates if happy posts actually go viral. If most dots are clustered on the left side (0.0 to 0.4 Positivity) but are high up, it proves that "Good News" doesn't sell—controversy drives engagement here.

5. Top 10 Most Viral Posts Context

Analyst Insight: Highlights the extreme outliers. The tone of these top 10 mega-viral posts often sets the culture for the entire community. Are the biggest influencers optimistic or completely toxic?

6. Sentiment Volatility Index

Analyst Insight: Tracks emotional turbulence. Massive spikes indicate days of extreme polarization where users were heavily split in intense warfare. Flat, low lines indicate boring, uniform consensus.

7. Toxicity vs Criticism

Analyst Insight: Groups negative scores into severity brackets. Determines if negative posts are just mildly critical (0.5 to 0.7 probability) or intensely furious and toxic (0.8 to 1.0 probability).

8. Enthusiasm vs Agreement

Analyst Insight: Groups positive scores into severity brackets. Shows if the community's optimistic posts are just mildly agreeable or fiercely enthusiastic, blindly supportive, and loyal.

9. Informational (Neutral) Flow

Analyst Insight: Tracks the flow of unbiased news, factual reporting, and dry questions over time. By separating this from opinionated rhetoric, we can see if the community is actually discussing facts or just fighting.

10. Active Posting Hours

Analyst Insight: Total posts aggregated by the time of day they were published in UTC. Reveals the peak active hours when the public comes online to engage in the discourse.

11. Systemic AI Bias Baseline

Analyst Insight: Exposes the fundamental baseline bias of the data. The AI scores every post. Even if a post is classified as "Neutral", it might have structural underlying negativity radiating throughout the language.

12. Detail vs Positivity

Analyst Insight: Compares character length against positivity. Answers the psychological question: "When users write massive, multi-paragraph essays, are they deeply appreciative, or are they writing furiously toxic rants?"

13. The Viral Inequality Curve

Analyst Insight: Illustrates the platform's attention inequality. Typically shows that 95% of posts get completely ignored (0-10 upvotes) while the top 5% go massively viral and monopolize the algorithm's attention.

14. Compounding Discussion Growth

Analyst Insight: The steepness of this running-total curve indicates raw momentum. A sudden vertical climb means an explosion in public interest, while a flattening curve means the topic has completely died out.

15. Cultural Weekly Rhythm

Analyst Insight: Discovers weekly behavioral trends. For instance, you can use this to determine if users are significantly more negative and combative on Mondays compared to the relaxing weekends.
